about

We solve problems.
We break systems.

H3XSEC is a competitive CTF team and security-focused community for people who want to build real offensive skills.

We work through challenges together, share knowledge, and improve through practice. From first solves to advanced exploitation, everyone is expected to contribute and learn.

Most activity happens on Discord: live collaboration during CTFs, focused channels per category, and a growing archive of writeups and resources.
